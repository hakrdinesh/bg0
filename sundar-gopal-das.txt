 His Grace Sundara Gopal Prabhu has been a disciple of 
His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Srila Prabhupada, 
Founder Acarya of ISKCON, known as the Hare Krishna Movement 
worldwide.

 Born in Singapore in 1955 to a humble family of Krishna Devotees, 
he graduated with a Diploma in Electrical Engineering from 
Singapore Polytechnic in 1976.

 He dedicated his life to carrying out the order of his spiritual 
master in spreading the teachings as laid out in the Bhagavad-gita 
and the Srimadh-Bhagavatam, after getting his Diksha (Spiritual 
Initiation) from Srila Prabhupada.

 Having 38 years of vast experience in this field, he wishes to 
reach out to you and share his experience to one and all in his 
simple and lucid style of presentation.
